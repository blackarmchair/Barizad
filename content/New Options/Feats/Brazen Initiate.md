_You’ve been exposed to or trained with relics of True Brass._
 
**Prerequisite:** Region: Satrapies of Varnash or DM permission
 
**Benefit:** +1 to AC vs spell-like abilities and +1 bonus on any one saving throw type (chosen when this feat is taken).
 
**Special:** If holding or wearing an item of True Brass, you gain a +2 bonus to saves against divine spells from outsiders.
