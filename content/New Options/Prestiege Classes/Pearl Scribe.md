_Wind-reader, oath-binder, and master of diplomatic dream-lore.__￼_(Enchanter/diviner hybrid; social control; navigation)￼  
These sages bind contracts with starlight ink and navigate dream-currents using mirrored sextants.￼  
**Hit Die**: d6
 
**Requirements:**  
To qualify to become a Pearl Scribe, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
 
**Skills:** Diplomacy 8 ranks, Knowledge (Geography) 4 ranks  
**Feat**: Spell Focus (Enchantment or Divination)  
**Special**: Must have inscribed a dream-map or traded with a spirit of the sea
 
**Class Skills:**  
The Pearl Scribe's class skills are Appraise, Bluff, Decipher Script, Diplomacy, Knowledge (Geography), Knowledge (Local), Knowledge (The Planes), Sense Motive, Speak Language, Spellcraft  
**Skill Points at Each Level**: 6 + Int modifier.

Class Features
 
**Silver Inscription (Su)**: Once per day (twice/day at 4th level), you may inscribe a creature’s name onto a polished silver tablet. For the next 24 hours, you gain a +2 insight bonus on all saves, skill checks, and opposed rolls against that creature. The creature is unaware of this effect.
 
**Dream-Compass (Su)**: You always know the direction to any location you have personally mapped or dream-walked to. This functions even across planes if the destination still exists.
 
**Mirror-Tongue (Su)**: You may speak with aquatic spirits, dreambound creatures, or illusions as though under a _Tongues_ spell. This ability is always active but only functions with these creature types.
 
**Inkbound Clause (Ex)**: Whenever you cast a divination or enchantment spell on a target after a successful opposed Diplomacy check, increase your effective caster level by +2 for that spell.
 
**Whisper of the Tides (Sp)**: Once per day, you may contact a spirit of the Mirror-Sea as a _Contact Other Plane_ spell. However, the entity may offer poetic or allegorical answers and always answers truthfully—if cryptically.
