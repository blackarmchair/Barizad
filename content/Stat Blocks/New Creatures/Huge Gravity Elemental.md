_Large masses of semi-solid gravitational anomalies shaped like vaguely humanoid forms. Their presence causes the battlefield to bend and twist with erratic force.__￼_  
**Huge Elemental (Air/Earth subtype, Extraplanar)**  
**Hit Dice**: 16d8+64 (136 hp)  
**Initiative**: +3
 
**Speed**: 40 ft., fly 60 ft. (perfect) _(appears to float by folding space around it)_  
**Armor Class**: 21 (-2 size, +3 Dex, +10 natural), touch 11, flat-footed 18
 
**Base Attack/Grapple**: +12/+27  
**Attack**: Slam +17 melee (2d8+7 + gravity pulse)  
**Full Attack**: 2 slams +17 melee (2d8+7 + gravity pulse)  
**Space/Reach**: 15 ft./15 ft.
 
**Special Attacks**: Gravity Pulse, Spatial Disruption  
**Special Qualities**: Damage reduction 5/—, elemental traits, gravity aura, darkvision 60 ft.
 
**Saves**: Fort +12, Ref +8, Will +8  
**Abilities**: Str 24, Dex 17, Con 19, Int 6, Wis 11, Cha 9  
**Skills**: Listen +8, Spot +9, Balance +12, Tumble +10  
**Feats**: Power Attack, Improved Bull Rush, Flyby Attack, Awesome Blow, Improved Initiative, Cleave

**Organization**: Solitary, pair, or cluster (3–5)  
**Challenge Rating**: 7  
**Treasure**: None  
**Alignment**: Always Neutral  
**Advancement**: 17–24 HD (Huge)  
**Level Adjustment**: —
 
**Special Abilities**
 
**Gravity Pulse (Su)**  
Whenever a gravity-warped elemental hits with a slam attack, the target must succeed on a **DC 20 Reflex save** or be **thrown 10 feet in a random direction** and fall **prone**. If the direction is into a wall or solid object, the target takes **2d6 additional damage**. This is a supernatural effect. The save DC is Constitution-based.
 
**Spatial Disruption (Su, 1/round)**  
As a **standard action**, the elemental targets a 10-ft.-radius area within 60 feet. All creatures in that area must make a **DC 18 Will save** or be _delayed_ in time, acting at **–5 initiative next round** and unable to take immediate or swift actions for 1 round.
 
**Gravity Aura (Ex)**  
The elemental creates a localized distortion field. All movement within **10 feet** of it is treated as **difficult terrain**, and any flying creature must make a **DC 15 Fly check** each round or descend 10 feet.
 
**Tactics**

- Uses **Flyby Attack** to strike and reposition erratically
- Opens with **Spatial Disruption** to stagger the party’s tempo
- Uses **Awesome Blow** to scatter frontline fighters
- Coordinates with others to split the party across fractured terrain
