**Ashfire (Su)**:  
_His blade burns not flesh but reason, time, and space._  
Whenever Sirr al-Nar hits with a touch attack:

- Deals 4d6 fire damage + 4d6 untyped damage
- 1/round triggers an automatic _greater dispel magic_ effect against one ongoing magical or divine effect
 
**Unwritten Prophecy (Su, 1/day)**  
_As a standard action, Sirr al-Nar may declare a target’s false destiny._  
Will save (DC 26) or be compelled to follow a false behavioral directive for 5 rounds.  
Failure causes:

- Behavioral override (e.g., forced charge, denial of healing)
- +2 to hit for Sirr al-Nar against that target
- Disobedience causes 4d6 nonlethal damage and 1 round of confusion
 
**Recalibrate Reality (Ex, 3/day)**  
Immediate action. May force a reroll of any d20 roll from the previous round.  
He chooses which result stands. Affects attacks, saves, checks, even spell penetration.
 
**Recursive Form (Ex)**  
As a standard action Sirr al-Narr can summon an array of mirror forms that cavort all around him copying his every movement with minor variation. This causes a 40% miss chance from partial phasing across timelines. A _true seeing_ spell reveals the real Sirr al-Narr. The mirror forms cannot do actual damage and are entirely incoporeal. The mirror forms dissipate after 3 rounds.  
￼Every “destroyed” mirror form grants Sir al-Narr:

- Fast healing 10 for 1 round
- Recovery of one expended spell-like ability (up to 6th level)
 
**Phase Transition (Ex, 1/day)**  
When reduced to 0 HP, Sirr al-Nar explodes in Ashfire and reforms at 40% HP.  
Upon reforming:

- Gains Fast Healing 5 (3 rounds)
- Ashfire Blade damage becomes 6d6 fire + 6d6 untyped
- Gains aura: Reality Feedback – all spellcasting within 15ft requires a Concentration check (DC 20 + spell level)
- After reforming, Sirr al-Nar's fast healing ends after 3 rounds. His body destabilizes after 5 rounds, reducing his AC and spell resistance by 2.
 
**TACTICS**

- Opening: Begins in negotiation or monologue. Casts _wall of force_ to divide the party, then _mass suggestion_ or _empowered fireball_.
- Mid-Fight: Uses _Unwritten Prophecy_ to disable a key PC, then exploits _recalibrate_ to control outcomes.
- Phase 2: After Phase Transition, moves into melee with Ashfire Blade and spams touch attacks. Pushes casters to waste actions failing checks due to his aura.
