**Litany of the Ninth Chain (Su, 1/day)**

- 40 ft. radius; Will DC 22 or:
    
    - –2 morale penalty on all d20 rolls
    - 20% divine spell failure
    - Limited to 1 action/round for 1d3 rounds
 
**Blood Is the Writ (Ex)**

- Sacrifice up to 30 HP to boost one spell’s CL and save DC by +1 per 10 HP
- Can also bypass SR with this effect
 
**Chains of False Revelation (Su, 3/day)**

- Will save DC 21 or suffer _geas-like compulsion_ for 5 rounds (e.g., must charge, may not retreat)
- Breaking command causes 4d6 Wisdom damage and confusion (1 round)
 
**Aura of the Inverted Flame (Ex)**

- Allies in 20 ft. gain +2 morale bonus on attacks and saves
- Enemies take –2 penalty on saves vs. fear, compulsion, and fate-based effects

- **Opener**: _Greater Command_ + _Blade Barrier_ to divide the field
