The Ifriqa arise from unions between mortals and djinn. They appear mostly human, but elemental halos betray their origin: a shimmer of heat, a glow of shadow, or hair that flickers like kindled threads. Many Ifriqa live under divine pacts or ancestral contracts, their lives shaped by bindings older than memory. Some are honored as bridges between worlds; others are feared as “walking war relics,” ticking with latent power. Proud and unpredictable, the Ifriqa carry within them both spark and storm—and are never truly unbound.￼  
Subtypes: Emberborn (fire), Windcalled (air), Quartz-Touched (earth), Drowned-Voiced (water/dream)
   

**In Game**:
 
**Type:** Outsider (Native, Elemental-Blooded)  
**LA:** +1  
**Ability Modifiers:** +2 Charisma, +2 Dexterity, –2 Constitution  
**Speed:** 30 ft.  
**Special Traits:**

- **Elemental Legacy:** Choose one elemental aspect at 1st level (Fire, Air, Earth, or Dream-Water). You gain a corresponding ability:
    
    - **Fire (Ashblood):** 1/day _Produce Flame_ as a spell-like ability (CL = HD)
    - **Air (Whirlskin):** +10 ft. movement speed; counts as feather fall at will if unarmored
    - **Earth (Quartzborn):** DR vs bludgeoning weapons equal to half HD (rounded up)
    - **Water/Dream (Drowned-Voiced):** 1/day _Hypnotism_ as a spell-like ability (CL = HD)
- **Djinni-Bound:** +2 bonus on Diplomacy checks with Outsiders and elementals
- **Unstable Lineage:** You radiate faint planar resonance detectable with _Detect Chaos_ or _Detect Magic_

**Drawback – Elemental Inheritance:** If reduced below 0 HP, roll d%. On 01–10, your elemental side flares unpredictably (GM’s choice: wild fire burst, ethereal shift, etc.)  
**Favored Class:** Sorcerer or Bard (depending on aspect)
