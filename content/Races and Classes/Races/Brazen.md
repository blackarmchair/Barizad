Forged in the celestial heart of Zahr al-Narās, the **Brazen** are living constructs made from **True Brass and other sacred alloys**, each etched with a divine fragment of forgotten purpose. Once tools of fate’s machinery, many awakened into full sapience only after the City's fall—haunted by “echo memories” of its final hours. Revered by some as monuments, resented by others as reminders of ruin, the Brazen now seek their own paths. A few long to “reboot” the old order; others crave nothing more than the freedom to reject it. Each Brazen bears both legacy and burden in every footstep.
 
**In Game**:  
**Type:** Construct (Living Construct)  
**LA:** +1  
**Ability Modifiers:** +2 Constitution, –2 Wisdom  
**Speed:** 30 ft.  
**Special Traits:**

- **Living Construct Traits:** Immune to poison, sleep effects, paralysis, disease, nausea, fatigue, exhaustion, energy drain. Not immune to mind-affecting effects. Healed by both cure spells and repair spells.
- **Celestine Echo:** You contain a fragment of Brass City directive code. Once per day, you may reroll a failed Will save vs compulsion or charm.
- **Reinforced Chassis:** Natural Armor +1.
- **Function Slot:** Choose a subroutine at 1st level (may reflavor as a background memory):
    
    - **Archivist:** +2 on Knowledge (History), may reroll 1 failed check/day
    - **Shield-Relay:** Immediate action once/day to grant adjacent ally +1 AC for 1 round
    - **Pulse Beacon:** 1/day _Light_ and _Message_ simultaneously (nonmagical)

**Drawback – Echo Memory:** Whenever you witness a Celestine relic, roll Will DC 13 or suffer a 1-round daze as ancient memory floods your mind.
