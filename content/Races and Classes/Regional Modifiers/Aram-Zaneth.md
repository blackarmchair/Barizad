_Fate-deniers and subverters of divine architecture.__￼_

- +1 Intelligence, -1 Charisma: Brilliant and cunning, but often unsettling or intense.￼
- +2 bonus on Knowledge (Arcana) and Use Magic Device.￼
- Gain SR = 5 + character level against divination spells or effects (can lower it willingly).￼
- May choose one divination spell they are always aware of when it is cast within 30 ft, even if not targeted. - –1 penalty on **saving throws against illusions**; they often over-analyze.
