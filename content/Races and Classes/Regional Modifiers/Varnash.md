_Imperial aristocrats blessed by True Brass and sovereign heritage._
 
- +1 Charisma, -1 Wisdom: Commanding and proud, but not always cautious.￼
- +2 bonus on Diplomacy and Craft (True Brass).￼
- Once per day, may reroll a failed saving throw vs mind-affecting effects (must keep second roll).￼
- –2 penalty on social rolls when visibly disheveled, unarmored, or bearing visible injuries.
