_Star-blooded mortals shaped by ritual, embalming, and soul-navigation._
 
- +1 Wisdom, -1 Strength: Introspective and spiritually attuned, but not always physically robust.￼
- +2 bonus on Knowledge (Religion) and Heal checks.￼
- Resist Sleep and Fear effects (+2 racial bonus on saves).￼
- Culturally reverent of burial traditions; desecrating the dead is a grave taboo (–2 on social rolls in their society if violated).
