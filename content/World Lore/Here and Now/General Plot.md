**Act I – Prophecies Walk**

1. First manifestations of fractured fate appear (duplicated people, contradictory pasts, time rifts).
2. The PCs recover the first gem and learn of its stabilizing—but reductive—effect.
3. Factions make contact, begin offering deals or threats.
 
**Act II – Dreams in Conflict**

1. Dungeons become battlefields as other groups seek the gems too.
2. Some True Dreams contradict each other—players must choose which to feed to the Furnace.
3. NPCs vanish or are overwritten based on choices made.
 
**Act III – Ascension or Annihilation**

1. Sirr al-Nar begins acting directly—his altered Furnace self tries to “overwrite” fate.
2. PCs can side with a faction, betray all, or unlock a third option: forge a new destiny.
3. The final dungeon isn’t just a fragment—it’s the Celestine Furnace itself.
