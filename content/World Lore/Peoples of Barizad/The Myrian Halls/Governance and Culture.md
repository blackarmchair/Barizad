Each Hall is ruled by a Prophet-King whose line is said to carry the blood-memory of the Sky-Prince Naram-Kel, the first to descend with flame in his breath and brass on his brow.
 
Rule is ritualistic, guided by prophetic signs, dream-portents, and astral observation, but also administrative—the Myrians are skilled architects, scribes, and astronomer-priests.￼

- Important matters are decided by the Circle of Celestial Descent, a council of high-ranking Lens Binders.￼
- Even common folk maintain ancestral shrines and recite portions of The Descent Chronicle, a semi-mythic text cataloging their skyborn heritage.
