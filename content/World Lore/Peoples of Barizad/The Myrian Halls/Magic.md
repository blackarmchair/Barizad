Myrian spellcasting is formal and lineage-based:￼

- Clerics invoke the Sky-Born through ancestor masks and consecrated staves.￼
- Wizards work from brass-bound burning tablets, tracing star-scripts that predict or invoke long-buried truths.￼
- Summoners are cautious—calling spirits only from within defined ancestral boundaries, or through blood-linked tokens.￼

They do not “invent” magic; they recover and restore what was forgotten in the fall.
 
The Burning Tablets

- These are not laws, but cosmic notes—flashes of past and future insight that fell from the sky in flame as the Brass City ruptured.￼
- Some are prophetic, others mnemonic, still others schematic records of celestial function.￼
- Most are interpreted ritually; some are planted beneath ziggurats to “seed” the right outcomes during floods, eclipses, or births.
