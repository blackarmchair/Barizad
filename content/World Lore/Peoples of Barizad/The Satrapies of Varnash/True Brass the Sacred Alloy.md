**“It does not rust. It does not burn. It reflects even godlight.”**
 
True Brass is the cornerstone of Varnathi belief, power, and identity.

- Said to be the original medium of celestial governance, it now serves as a shield against divine influence and a conductor of magical intent.￼
- Each noble house guards a distinct relic of True Brass: a blade, circlet, mirror, or seal. These are believed to “anchor the self” against fate.￼
- Priests and artisans spend lifetimes polishing, tuning, and aligning True Brass to capture and reflect desired outcomes.￼

Magical Properties (in-game effects):

- Renders the bearer resistant to mind-altering effects, fate-binding, and certain celestial magics￼
- Can refract prophecy—confusing or deflecting attempts to divine the owner’s future￼
- Resonates with Brass City mechanisms, allowing access or disruption of Celestine artifacts
