The Thalassocracy of Nisarun is a coastal league of merchant-kings, wind-ship captains, and veiled seers. Built upon slender isles, reef-halls, and brine-bitten cliff cities, the Nisari define themselves not by territory, but by routes—trade winds, dream tides, and arcane sea-lanes traced across both sky and water.
 
Where others seek prophecy or conquest, the Nisari seek leverage. Their mastery of negotiation, esoteric trade, and nautical sorcery has made them essential and resented by every land power.
 
They never claim neutrality. They sell it.
