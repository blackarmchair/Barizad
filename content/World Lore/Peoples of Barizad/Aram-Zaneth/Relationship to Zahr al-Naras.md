The people of Aram-Zaneth claim a forgotten lineage, insisting that their ancestors once served in the deepest and most secret temples of the Brass City—keepers of hidden truths and forbidden rites. When Zaneth committed his great heresy, seeking to unbind fate and offer mortals the power to shape their own destinies, these faithful were cast out alongside him. They did not fall in disgrace, they say, but were chosen to carry the true flame into exile.
 
To them, the scattered fragments of the Brass City—those twisted ruins that warp time and bleed possibility—are not mere wreckage, but sacred keys. Within each lies a whisper of lost divinity, a clue to the great undoing of the cosmic order. They do not seek to restore what once was, but to claim their rightful place among the stars, no longer as servants of fate, but as its rebels, breakers, and heirs.
 
Their most fervent pursuit is the collection of dream-gems, the crystallized remnants of prophecy and divine vision once housed in the Celestine Furnace. The Aram-Zanethi believe these gems hold the power to resurrect Zaneth in full, not as a chained god, but as a burning sovereign. They dream of a final reckoning, when the false sun of mechanized destiny will be consumed in sacred flame, and Zaneth’s light will illuminate a world remade in suffering, freedom, and truth. No price is too high. No atrocity too far. For them, this is not vengeance—it is fulfillment.
