The Emerald Courts believed themselves aligned with the Brass City during its zenith—mirroring its divine machinery with temples of geometry and flame.
 
- Brass sigils were built into tombs to redirect soul-energies toward celestial gates.￼
- Some sects now claim their founding ancestors were stewards of the city’s lowest vaults, not rulers, but custodians of forgotten calendars and failed prophecies.￼
- There is growing unrest over whether their afterlife rituals still function, now that the engine of fate is broken.
