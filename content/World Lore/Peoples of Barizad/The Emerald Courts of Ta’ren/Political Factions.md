- The Court of Embalmed Radiance
    
    - Traditionalists who believe in preserving old rites to await a new alignment.
    - Back the God-Queen and her priesthood.￼
- The Cult of the Hollow Star
    
    - Heretics who claim the Mirror Falcon has gone blind.
    - Call for disruptive reforms—or even an alliance with Aram-Zaneth to “forge a new ascension."￼
- The Obelisk Restorationists
    
    - Engineers and astrologers seeking to reignite the brass geomancy embedded in ancient tombs.
    - Occasionally recruit adventurers to recover fragments of the Celestine Flame.
