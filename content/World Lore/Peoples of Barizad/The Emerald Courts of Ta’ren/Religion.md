The Emerald Courts revere a pantheon of abstract deities, celestial principles embodied in animal-aspected spirits. Foremost among them is:
 
- The Mirror Falcon
    
    - A divine avatar of judgment, alignment, and soul-weighing.
    - Depicted as a falcon with mirrored feathers and a single unblinking eye.
    - Said to perch atop the brass obelisks that mark the way to the afterlife.
    - Worshipped by high judges, executioners, and dream-clerics.
    - Believed to refract the soul’s last dream into the path it must walk after death.
 
Other divine concepts include:

- Seshmehet, Keeper of Breath – frog goddess of embalming and stillness
- Dja-at, the Time-Sifter – a veiled cobra who filters memory from soul
- Khem-Abes, the Dream-Marked – a monkey who sculpts the next world from mortal dreams
