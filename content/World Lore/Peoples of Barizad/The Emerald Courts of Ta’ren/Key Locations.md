Asmarat (Capitol)  
Asmarat derives from ancient liturgical Ta’reni meaning _"Reflected Ascent"_ or _"The Rising Mirror."_ Also called _Nehket-Aset_ ("Heart of the River") by locals
 
Overview  
Asmarat sits in a great oxbow of the River Aset. Its skyline bristles with brass obelisks and mirror-domed temples, all aligned to celestial nodes once calculated by dream-astronomers of the old age.

- At its center rises the _Pyramid of Alignment_, a sacred tomb-temple where each God-Queen is interred—yet also said to ascend, their spirits measured and launched through rituals of soul-geometry.￼
- ~~The~~ _Throne of Breath_~~, the God-Queen’s palace, is carved directly into the cliff-face, with windows of polished crystal overlooking the entire city and river valley.~~￼
- The _Veiled Forum_, a dream-projection amphitheater, hosts debates and judgments where litigants submit their sleeping minds to astral interrogation.￼
- The _Sepulchral Canal_ runs beneath the city—a slow-moving waterway where embalmer-priests ferry wrapped dead through stations of ritual and mnemonic purification.
 
Civic Identity  
Asmarat is not just a city—it is a divine machine, built to guide the souls of the Ta'reni along the correct path of afterlife and ascension.￼

- Every street follows star-paths etched in brass, adjusted in the aftermath of the Brass City's fall—though no one agrees which new constellations are true.￼
- Caste-districts echo celestial functions:
    
    - The Kheru Quarter (soul-cultivators and river farmers) is lush and ritually irrigated.
    - The Sheshnet Vaults house embalmer-philosophers in necropolis-labs.
    - The Nemet Spires are home to the star-watchers and dream-casters.￼
- At night, the city glows from mirrorlight fires—refractions of fire through mirrored towers that flicker like constellations reborn.
 
Modern Tensions  
Once a city of certainty and soul-alignment, Asmarat now stands fractured by prophecy and disbelief:￼

- The Refraction Crisis: Mirror-Seers report increasing distortions in dream-visions. Some blame “echoes from the Vaults,” others whisper of a False Falcon roosting where the true one once perched.￼
- The Hollow Processions: Each season, more dead are returned from the tomb-canals unaligned—their dream-flasks empty, their embalming glyphs corrupted. Secretly, some are stored in hidden vaults beneath the palace.￼
- Factional Influence:
    
    - The Court of Embalmed Radiance continues its rites unchanged—praying that stability will return.
    - The Cult of the Hollow Star gathers at cracked obelisks in the Outer Ring, offering dreams to strange lights that rise from beneath.
    - The Obelisk Restorationists rebuild old soul-circuitry in the undercity, hoping to bypass failed rituals and directly contact the dream-engine that once governed fate.
