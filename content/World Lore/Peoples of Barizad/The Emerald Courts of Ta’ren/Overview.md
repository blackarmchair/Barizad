The Emerald Courts are a resplendent, theocratic river civilization centered along the River Aset, a lifegiving current that winds through obsidian cliffs, emerald fields, and alabaster necropolises. The kingdom is ruled by a divine bloodline descended from fallen star-metal—said to be shards of prophecy itself.This ancient realm once sought ascension through death, not as an escape from life, but as a refinement of the soul, aligning it with the stellar order imposed by Zahr al-Narās. Today, its traditions are fractured—part faithful, part apostate, part unknowingly corrupted by forgotten links to the lowest vaults of the fallen city and subversion by Aram-Zaneth.
