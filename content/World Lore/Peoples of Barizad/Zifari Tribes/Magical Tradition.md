Zifari magic is dream-based, drawn from sky, sand, and memory. It is never written. To write a spell is to _trap it_, which the Zifari see as profane.
 
Common magical paths:

- Stormsingers: Wind-priests who use the air to speak with spirits and scatter illusions.
- Mirage-Binders: Illusionists and trackers who shape shimmering terrain and displace pursuit.
- Ash-Readers: Dream seers who interpret patterns in fire-coal and starlight, giving guidance through riddles.
 
Zifari relics often include:

- Braids of wind-twine, storing whispered messages.
- Starborn sand from fallen meteors.
- Voice-jugs, which replay the final words of an ancestor.
