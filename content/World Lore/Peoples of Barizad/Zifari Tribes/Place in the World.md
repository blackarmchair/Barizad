The Zifari act as guides across dream-touched wastelands, willing to travel near Brass Fragments where others dare not go.
 
However, they are viewed with suspicion by some and looked-down upon by most as barbaric, dirty, uncivilized, and untrustworthy.

- Aram-Zaneth sees them as dangerous heretics who "lack the strength to impose their will".
- Djinn-warlords sometimes hunt them for their ancestral knowledge of pre-Brass prophecy.
