**Based on:** _The Tale of the Three Apples_
 
**Dungeon Concept:** A gladiator vault once used for efreeti amusement, now corrupted by fire-beasts and haunted champions.
 
**Dungeon Highlights:**

- Arena structure with escalating combat trials
- Between rounds, party enters dream-arenas that affect the waking fight
- Final boss: _Amarion, the Ruby Hound_—a mutated undead beast-druid
 
**Dream-Gem:** _The Hunger for Justice_
