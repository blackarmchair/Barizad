**Based on:** _The Fisherman and the Djinn_
 
**Dungeon Concept:** A broken atrium of the Brass City where wishes—once refined like alchemical gold—now fester in warped crucibles. The dungeon tempts, grants, and twists the desires of all who enter.
 
**Dungeon Highlights:**

- At the entrance, each PC is offered one “true wish” by a bound relic—but consequences are delayed, hidden, or ironic
- Rooms reflect distorted versions of players’ stated goals, creating bespoke enemies, illusions, or boons
- The central chamber contains the _Well of Wanting_, where the party must choose whether to extract the Dream-Gem… at a price
 
**Final Encounter:**

- _Zhadir the Ungranted_, a genie-phantasm fused from centuries of incomplete or revoked wishes, seeks to “complete” the party’s desires—whether they want it or not
 
**Dream-Gem:** _The Desire That Could Not Die_
