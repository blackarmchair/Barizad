**Based on:** _The Tale of the False Caliph_
 
**Location:** Beneath a ruined oasis city, partially buried in sand
 
**Theme:** Identity collapse, impersonation, fractured memory
 
**Premise:**  
A brass observatory that once charted destinies now flickers between timelines. A powerful figure claiming to be the “Rightful Caliph” rules inside, his followers mindless reflections echoing his delusion. The vault generates false versions of people—duplicates of the PCs, NPCs, or even historical figures—who argue over who is real.
 
**Dungeon Features:**

- Memory loops and distorted dialogue trees
- Mirror-clones of the PCs as boss encounters
- A prophecy-gem at the center, locked behind a trial of truth versus certainty
