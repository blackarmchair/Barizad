**Based on:** _The Tale of the Porter's Bargain_ and general bazaar tales
 
**Location:** A fragment of the Grand Market of Zahr al-Narās, stuck in a permanent twilight
 
**Theme:** Desire, consequence, transactional destiny
 
**Premise:**  
This dungeon appears as a bizarre, living bazaar where time is currency and memory is sold. Merchants offer wondrous magical items in exchange for future deeds or past decisions. The PCs must navigate shifting stalls, misleading contracts, and cursed wares to retrieve a prophecy-gem that no one remembers owning.
 
**Dungeon Features:**

- “Contract” encounters: magical trades alter the party’s future
- Time loop room: one decision must be replayed until “truth” is chosen
- Final encounter: an incarnate debt-collector composed of forgotten promises
