1. Description
    
    1. The room is spartan and narrow.￼
    2. In the center of the far wall is a simple pillar made of pure brass￼
    3. The pillar has two hand-shaped imprints carved into the side (but made for much larger than human hands)￼
2. Mechanics
    
    1. Placing one's hand into the indents the far wall is revealed to be illusory￼
    2. An archway is revealed; the doorway beneath is sealed by a slab of pure brass￼
    3. Inscribed on the brass door is the following (in Celestial):￼￼ _In darkness it starts and in darkness it ends._ _￼_ _It burns as it rises and as it descends.__￼_ _The in-between places glow warmly, you know,__￼_ _But not like the apex, the highest it goes__￼_ _Follow its path as it travels above, From start until finish, all colors thereof._ _￼_
    4. On the pillar 11 crystal orbs seem to appear:
        
        1. 2 Dark Purple
        2. 2 Red
        3. 2 Orange
        4. 2 Green
        5. 2 Blue
        6. 1 Yellow￼
    5. A successful Search or Spot check (DC 14) reveals that there are empty slots in the archway that the gems would fit perfectly.￼
    6. Placing the crystals in the openings in the arch in a pattern mimicking the sun’s path from dawn until dusk (dark purple, red, orange, yellow, orange, red, dark purple) will open a passage underneath the arch.￼
