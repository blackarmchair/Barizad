_Archivists of prophecy and celestial resonance through written record._
 
- +1 Intelligence, -1 Dexterity: Precise but physically deliberate.
- +2 bonus on Decipher Script and Knowledge (History).
- May cast Read Magic at will as a spell-like ability.
- Must take a full-round action to draw or read any written item or scroll unless a DC 15 Concentration check is passed.
