_Wanderers of the deep desert, singers of uncharted stars, and keepers of the old rhythm.___

- +1 Wisdom, -1 Intelligence: Insightful and intuitive, but less bound to academic structure.
- +2 bonus on Survival and Perform (Oratory).
- Gain Endurance as a bonus feat at 1st level.
- Cannot use any spell with a written component (scrolls, runes, etc.) unless it has been translated orally by a known source.
