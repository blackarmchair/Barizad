The standard Cleric domains are replaced with the following:
 
|   |   |   |   |
|---|---|---|---|
|**Domain**|**Affiliated Deity / Power**|**Factions / Peoples**|**Symbolic Associations**|
|**Brass**|_The Celestine Furnace_ (divine engine); **Zahr al-Narās**|Myrian Halls, Hollowforged, Satrapies of Varnash (traditionalists)|Order, celestial gears, binding oaths, ancient enforcement|
|**Dream**|_Nimurah_, goddess of the mirror-sea; _The Drowned Library_ (conceptual)|Nimari, Zifari, Emerald Courts (rites), Nisarun|Illusion, prophecy, sleep, the unknown future, tidal fate|
|**Trade**|_Yenazra_, goddess of negotiation and wind-ink contracts; _The Market Moon_|Nisarun, Satrapies of Varnash, rogue Aram-Zaneth sects|Bargains, written word, wealth, debt, hidden gain|
|**Reckoning**|_The Mirror Falcon_ (celestial avatar of judgment); _Zaneth_ (vengeance aspect)|Emerald Courts, Aram-Zaneth zealots, exile inquisitors|Justice, retribution, divine accounting, exile|
|**Ash**|_The Ember That Speaks_, an apocalyptic fire-entity; funerary cults of the Emerald Courts|Emerald Courts, Zifari, flame-haunted ruin hunters|Burned prophecy, destruction, funeral purity, endings|
|**Echo**|_The Resonant Vault_ (memory construct); _The First Whisper_|Myrian archivists, Hollowforged, Brass archivists|Memory, recursive magic, forgotten voices, the weight of history|
|**Fate**|_Zahr al-Narās_, personified as the Watchmaker; _The Spinner of Threads_ (folk deity)|Brass-loyalists, Zifari bards, seers of many nations|Inevitability, cosmic design, prophecy, the unbroken thread|
