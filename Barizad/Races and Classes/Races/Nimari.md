The Nimari are a people claimed by Nimurah’s mirror-sea, often born during high tide, dream-storms, or moments when the boundaries between sleep and prophecy thin. Their eyes gleam like still water, and their voices carry harmonic undertones that hint at deeper meanings. Shaped by a culture as fluid as the tides, Nimari move through identities and allegiances with grace, sometimes to the frustration of more rigid societies. They resent being “fixed” into roles or names and are often drawn to mysticism, artistry, diplomacy, or double lives. Some believe their souls reflect hidden truths others cannot face—and fear or envy them accordingly.  
**In Game**:  
**Type:** Humanoid (Tideborn, Extraplanar)  
**LA:** +0  
**Ability Modifiers:** +2 Charisma, –2 Strength  
**Speed:** 30 ft. (Swim 20 ft. if born near deep waters)  
**Special Traits:**

- **Echo Voice:** 1/day, reroll any failed Bluff or Diplomacy check. Must accept second result.
- **Tide-Blooded:** +2 bonus on saves vs enchantment spells and effects.
- **Dream-Drifter:** +2 bonus on Knowledge (The Planes) and Perform (Song or Oratory).
- **Mirror-Born:** Once per day, you may cast _Silent Image_ as a spell-like ability (CL = HD).
- **Languages:** Automatically speak Dreamtongue and Aquan, in addition to Common.

**Drawback – Mercurial Soul:** –2 penalty on Sense Motive checks against lawful-aligned creatures. Your fluid identity makes it hard to concentrate.
