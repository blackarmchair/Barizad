1. The Dahrin:
    
    1. Efreeti Aristocracy
    2. Known for being unfeeling and immutable.
    3. See themselves as vessels of the Celestine Will, not as individual rulers with agency.
    4. Judges and law-givers come exclusively from this class
    5. The only glass of genie with individual agency
2. The Jannat al-Ruh
    
    1. Scholars & Oracles
    2. Interpreters of the Great Harmonic Script, capable of perceiving discordant timelines.
    3. Their mercy lay in recalculating fate, not sparing lives.
    4. Believed in predetermined enlightenment: only those destined to understand truth ever could.
3. The Kibrin
    
    1. Laboring Castes & Golem-Builders
    2. Built without will but maintained perfect alignment with their designated roles.
    3. Saw emotion and deviation as faulty metallurgy.
    4. Their work was holy—to sustain the city was to sustain existence.
