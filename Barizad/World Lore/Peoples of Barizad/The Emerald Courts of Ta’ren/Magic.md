The Emerald Courts are masters of spirit magic, mummification, and dreamcraft. Brass, star-metal, and embalming resin are treated as arcane catalysts.
 
Magical Traditions:

- Dream Scribes: Sorcerers and wizards who write spells into sleeping minds, harvesting them at dawn.
- Embalmer-Clerics: Preserve and empower the dead, binding divine breath into their wrappings.
- Mirror-Seers: Use polished brass mirrors to project soul-echoes into the Astral Planes.
 
Notable Practices:

- Soul-Pyramid Ascension: Tombs are designed not to contain, but to launch the soul through carefully calculated ritual geometries.
- Memory Harvesting: Elders’ dreams are ritually preserved in star-flasks, then consumed by successors for wisdom.
- Refraction Trials: Major decisions are submitted to mirror-judges, who reflect the will of the gods through controlled dream-prophecy.
