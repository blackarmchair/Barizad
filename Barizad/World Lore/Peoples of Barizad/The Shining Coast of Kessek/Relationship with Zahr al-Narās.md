The Nisari view Zahr al-Narās as a sunken investment—a thing to mine, not to mourn.

- The Nisari maintain deliberate neutrality with post-Celestine powers, particularly Efreeti warlords.
- They broker access to fragment sites, transport valuable relics between factions, and occasionally “misplace” cargo for profit or leverage.
- Many Efreet find them useful—but never trustworthy.
