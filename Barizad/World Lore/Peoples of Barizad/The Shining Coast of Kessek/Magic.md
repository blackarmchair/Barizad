The Nisari combine astronomy, dream-divination, and elemental windcraft to sail across both seas and sorceries.

- Their wind-ships use bound air elementals, rigged with mirror-kites and brass-thread sails to catch ethereal currents.
- Astronomer-scribes map dream-currents, non-physical tides that drift between the Material, Ethereal, and Dream realms.
- They speak of the Deep Below, a sleeping reflection of the ocean where lost truths and unanchored memories gather.
