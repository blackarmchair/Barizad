Nisarun dominates trade in:

- Brass fragments salvaged from fallen sky-shards
- Dream-gems, volatile minerals used to bind fate, memory, or visions
- Star-salt, an alchemical reagent gathered only during astral tides
- Licenses of passage—contracts sold to rival empires granting safe access to Celestine ruins (often double-sold to all sides)

They also traffic in whispers, myth-debts, and sealed truths—concepts brokered like commodities, often with long-term consequences.
