1. Titles:
    
    1. The Father of Flame
    2. The Inverted Light
    3. He Who Was Bound
    4. The God-Forged God
2. Cosmic Role
    
    1. Zaneth is not a creator deity in the traditional sense. Rather, he is a _former servant or creation of the Celestial Order_—perhaps an archon, seraph, or automaton of the Celestine Furnace—who rebelled, claiming the gods of heaven fear mortal agency.
    2. According to Aram-Zaneth doctrine:
        
        1. The Celestine Order enslaved time and destiny to maintain their perfect, golden world.
        2. Zaneth fractured prophecy deliberately, allowing mortals to shape it for themselves.
        3. For this crime, he was cast out, shackled in burning chains.
        4. His fall cracked the Brass City—the first blow in its eventual collapse.
    3. Philosophy
        
        1. Zaneth is not worshipped _as a god in the normal sense_—his divinity is the result of apotheosis through struggle.
        2. This theology frames Aram-Zaneth as a gnostic rebellion against celestial determinism
        3. Zaneth represents _becoming_—a fire that devours all imposed form so that "truth" can be forged from ash.
3. Alignment and Domains
    
    1. Alignment: NE
    2. Domains: Knowledge, Fire, Trickery
    3. Typical Worshipers: The people of Aram-Zaneth, Warlocks, misanthropic and dysgenic cultists
