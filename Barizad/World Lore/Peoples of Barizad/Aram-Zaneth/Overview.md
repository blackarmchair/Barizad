Aram-Zaneth is a shadowed remnant of the ancient peoples who dwelled in the land long before the Brass City imposed its mechanized vision of fate.
 
It is a theocracy of scattered city-states, each ruled by priest-kings and witch-queens bound together by ancient blood pacts.
 
Unlike empires forged through conquest, Aram-Zaneth is unified by a shared reverence for ancestral worship, sacrificial rites, and a sacred hunger for the forbidden knowledge buried with the Brass City. To them, the city's fall is not a tragedy but a divine opportunity—the long-awaited return of the gods beneath the earth, who now whisper through blood and fire.
