The Myrians see Zahr al-Narās not as a god—but as their lost celestial homeland.

- They believe that fragments of its great memory core are buried across the world, waiting to be retrieved and reconnected.
- Some factions hope to restore the city, or at least recreate a new descent cycle, one in which the Sky-Born may guide the world once more.

However, others fear that Sirr al-Nar is a false heir—a parasite wearing prophecy’s skin.
