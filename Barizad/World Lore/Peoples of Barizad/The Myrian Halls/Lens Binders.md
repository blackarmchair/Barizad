The Lens-Binders are a sacred order of judges schooled in ancient traditions and methods of interpreting various esoteric omens.
 
They use:

- Brass mirrors, etched with moving glyphs
- Fragments of dream-gems, suspended in oil or fire
- Tablet-staves, engraved with ancestral memory cycles
 
They read these tools to determine when the past should be repeated, which futures are safe to call forth, or how to navigate around broken paths left by the Brass City's fall.
