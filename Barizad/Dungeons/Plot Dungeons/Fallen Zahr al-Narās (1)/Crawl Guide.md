# Fallen Zahr al-Narās (Floor 1) — Crawl Guide
*Levels 5–7 | EL 7–9 | Party of Four*

## General Features

**Construction Material.** Industrial forge-works — hewn brass walls, iron-reinforced floors, brass piping along ceilings and walls. Everything is built to efreeti scale: doorways are 10 feet tall, corridors 10 feet wide. The construction is functional, not decorative — this was the working district of a divine engine.

**Ceiling Height.** Corridors are 10 feet high. Chambers vary: the Gasworks is 40 feet to the cracked pipe junction, the Crucible Walk spans a vast open pit, the Processional is a broad avenue open to a distant ceiling, and the Furnace Shaft is a 200+ foot vertical drop.

**Lighting.** Darkness, broken by environmental glow. The Gasworks has no light. The Crucible Walk is lit orange-white by molten slag below. The Frozen Processional is lit by suspended spell effects frozen in time. The Molten Floodgates glow from brass channels. The Furnace Shaft is pitch black. Creatures without Darkvision or light sources are effectively blind in corridors and the Gasworks.

## Crawl Strategy Notes

**Marching Order.** Corridors are 10 feet wide — double-file is standard. The Crucible Walk's catwalks are only 5 feet wide, forcing single-file with Balance checks (DC 10) at full speed. Plan transitions between formations.

**Visibility.** Light sources are critical. Torches last 1 hour (6 stretches). Everburning torches or Light spells are strongly recommended. The Gasworks is filled with combustible gas — **open flames will detonate the room** (8d6 fire, Reflex DC 15 half). A party relying on torches must extinguish them before entering. Sunrods, Darkvision, or Light cast on a non-flammable object are safe alternatives.

**Spot/Listen.** Note each PC's modifiers. Key thresholds:
- Spot +3 or better: notices the gas haze in the Gasworks before entering
- Listen +5 or better: hears the Fire Giant working valves in the Floodgates before the party is seen
- Spot/Search +8 or better: notices the brass ring anchor point in the Furnace Shaft (DC 12) without dedicating a stretch to searching

## Stretch Pacing

The five chambers are connected by brass corridors. Travelling between chambers costs 1 stretch per corridor. The party can encounter the chambers in any order except the Furnace Shaft, which comes last and leads to Floor 2.

### Room-by-Room Estimates

| Chamber | Estimated Stretches | Key Timing |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Corridors (between chambers) | 1 each | Roll for wandering encounters. |
| The Shattered Gasworks (EL 7) | 3–5 | Pipe repair takes 10 min (1 stretch). Gas clears in 10 min after repair (1 stretch). Bellows + ignition is a 3rd stretch minimum. Toxic gas Fortitude DC 14 every 10 min = every stretch. |
| The Crucible Walk (EL 8) | 2–4 | South ventricle repair takes 10 min (1 stretch). Heat escalation compresses time once ventricles close — shifts from stretch-scale to round-scale. |
| The Frozen Processional (EL 9) | 1–3 | Can be resolved in 1 stretch with a DC 25 Sleight of Hand check (risky). If stasis breaks, combat replaces stretch tracking until resolved. Exploration of side chambers adds 1 stretch each. |
| The Molten Floodgates (EL 8) | 1–2 | Primarily a combat encounter — time pressure is the Fire Giant opening valves, not stretch pacing. Resolve in initiative, then return to stretches. |
| The Furnace Shaft (EL 7) | 2–4 | Descent planning takes 1 stretch. Crane operation takes 1 stretch (or 10 min studying controls on a failed check = 2 stretches). Side chamber exploration adds 1 stretch each. |

**Total estimated stretches for Floor 1:** 12–24 (2–4 hours of in-game time)

### Relevant Activities (3.5e)

- **Search Area.** Essential in the Gasworks (Search DC 18 for the sealed cabinet), Furnace Shaft (Search DC 12 for the anchor ring), and Frozen Processional (to examine side chambers before triggering the unfreeze).
- **Keep Watch.** Critical during any repair work (Gasworks pipe, Crucible Walk ventricle). A watcher can warn of approaching wandering monsters.
- **Take Your Time.** Taking 20 on the Gasworks pipe repair (Strength DC 20) isn't possible — it requires holding with both hands while someone else secures it. Taking 20 on Climb checks (DC 15) is possible but costs 2 stretches per ascent.
- **Helping Out.** Aid Another on Strength checks (pipe repair, valve operation) is the intended design for several rooms.
- **Casting a Spell.** Ritual casting (10 min) fits neatly into 1 stretch. Relevant rituals: Detect Magic (to examine the hourglass), Comprehend Languages (to read the Kibrin journal in the Shaft).

## Wandering Monster Table

Wandering monsters patrol the corridors between chambers. They do not enter chambers — the environmental hazards keep them out.

**Encounter Frequency.** Constant — roll 1d6 at the end of every stretch spent in a corridor.
**Encounter Likelihood.** Unlikely — on a 6 only, roll on the table.
**Encounter Difficulty.** Trivial to Challenging — these should tax resources, not threaten a TPK. Floor 1 is about teaching dungeon-crawl discipline.
