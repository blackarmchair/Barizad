_Adapted from: The Slag Pits (Broadsheet 4.02)_

1. **Description**

    1. A series of narrow brass catwalks bridge a vast pit of molten slag — waste heat from the Celestine Furnace, still churning centuries after the Fall
    2. At the chamber's center, suspended by massive chains, hangs a brass sphere 10 feet in diameter: a heat-exchange node that once regulated temperature across an entire city district
    3. Two side chambers (north and south) house ventricle mechanisms that funnel excess heat away. Both must be closed to force enough thermal energy into the node to reactivate it
    4. The heat is immediate and oppressive. The brass catwalks are warm to the touch. The slag below glows white-orange.

2. **Mechanics**

    1. **Scorching Heat**: At the end of every 10 minutes a creature spends in this chamber, that creature must make a Fortitude save (DC 12) or take 1d6 points of nonlethal damage and become fatigued. Creatures with fire resistance reduce the DC by their resistance value. Creatures with fire immunity automatically succeed.
    2. **Escalation**: Each time the party closes a ventricle, the heat intensifies:
        1. _One ventricle closed_: the Fortitude save must be made every minute (10 rounds) instead of every 10 minutes
        2. _Both ventricles closed_: the save must be made every round. The node reactivates in 5 rounds, at which point the heat stabilizes and the exit unseals.
    3. **The Catwalks**: The catwalks are 5 feet wide with no railings. A creature that is bull rushed, knocked prone by damage, or fails a Balance check while moving at full speed (DC 10) risks falling. A creature that goes over the edge may attempt a Reflex save (DC 15) to grab the edge and hang on.
    4. **Molten Slag**: Any creature that falls in takes 10d6 fire damage per round. Objects take the same damage.
    5. **North Ventricle**: In good condition. A creature adjacent to the valve can close it as a full-round action (no check required).
    6. **South Ventricle**: Badly damaged. A successful Craft (blacksmithing) or Knowledge (architecture/engineering) check (DC 15) followed by 10 minutes of repair work restores it to working order. It can then be closed as a full-round action.
    7. **Protective Gear**: A locked storage alcove (Open Lock DC 20, or break: Hardness 10, 30 hp) near the chamber entrance contains two sets of heavy brass-plated coveralls. While wearing a set, a creature's speed is halved but it gains fire resistance 10.

3. **Encounters**

    1. The north ventricle chamber contains 2 **Salamanders** (MM 219) who have made a nest in the heat vent. They attack anything that approaches the valve.
    2. A **Large Fire Elemental** (MM 99) patrols the catwalks, emerging from the slag pit every 1d4 minutes. It attacks non-fire creatures on sight but ignores the salamanders.

4. **Treasure**

    1. In the storage alcove alongside the protective gear: a Masterwork Heavy Steel Shield with fire resistance 5 (as _Shield of Fire Resistance_, a minor custom item worth ~4,000 gp)
    2. The salamander nest contains: a cracked brass orrery worth 500 gp to a collector, a potion of Protection from Energy (Fire), and a _+1 Longspear_ with Ignan runes (the tip glows faintly warm)
    3. If the node is reactivated and examined (Search DC 16): a fist-sized True Brass ingot (component for crafting fire-resistant equipment, worth 1,200 gp)
