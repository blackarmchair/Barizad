1. **Description**
    
    1. A wide, open space with levitating chunks of buildings — the ruined city shattered into orbit
    2. You see a clear path by jumping three times from one large piece of debris to another and then to a hallway at the top of the room
    3. Gravity changes erratically as you move about the room making flying/jumping from stone-to-stone both required and difficult
    4. 3x Huge Gravity Elementals lurk among the debris
2. **Mechanics**
    
    1. **The hard way up**:
        
        1. Jumping from chunk to chunk is a crapshoot; normally, an Acrobatics check (DC 15) would be sufficient to make the leap but the rapid fluctuations in gravity give each attempt a 25% miss chance. Each piece of debris is 20ft, 40ft, and 60ft off the ground, respectively.
        2. Upon successfully jumping, the player must make a climb check (DC 12) to climb to the top of the debris section
    2. **The easy way up**:
        
        1. Flying. A DC15 fly check is required each time the gravity fluctuates (3x 1/4 chances on the way up)
3. **Encounters**
    
    1. 3x [Huge Gravity Elementals](Huge%20Gravity%20Elemental.md) await the PCs here
