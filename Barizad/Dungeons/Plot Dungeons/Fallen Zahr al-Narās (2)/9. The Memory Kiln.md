This was once a place where the Flamebound left behind memories, either to be burned or engraved into the city’s “fate archive.”

### Flavor Text

> You step into a quiet chamber of warmth and silence. The air is thick with the smell of scorched incense and old brass. Two alcoves face each other like mirrored shrines. Each contains a seated statue with an empty bowl where its heart would be. Faint trails of soot climb the walls like writing unspooled.
> 
> On the far wall, script in Celestine glyphs reads:
> "Only what is burned can be remembered."

### Functionality
- Players may each sacrifice a memory, spell, or item to the bowl
- Doing so causes a brief hallucinatory vision of a moment from the city's history (or their own possible future)
- Mechanically, sacrificing:
	- A spell slot: Gain +1 insight bonus to saves for 1 hour
	- A valuable item: Gain a permanent +1 bonus to a Knowledge check of your choice related to the city's lore
	- A narrative memory: No mechanical bonus, but powerful roleplay prompt

### Bonus
Knowledge (History or Arcana DC 25) reveals this room was originally designed to "feed memory to the Furnace" as an act of tribute or control.
