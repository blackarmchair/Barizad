Purpose

- Set the tone: grandeur, disorientation, danger
- Foreshadow the city’s fate and Sirr al-Nar’s madness
- Introduce traversal challenges (gravity, time)
- Offer the first _true dream_ or key to continue deeper  
Themes

- Distorted physics (gravity shifts, floating brass)
- Solar ruins and divine wreckage
- Partial reality — pieces of the level “skip” or replay out of sync
- Warning echoes of what’s to come
