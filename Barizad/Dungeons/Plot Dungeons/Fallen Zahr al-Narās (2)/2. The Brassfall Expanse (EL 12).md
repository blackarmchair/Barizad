## Description
- A wide, open space with levitating chunks of buildings — the ruined city shattered into orbit
- You see a clear path by jumping three times from one large piece of debris to another and then to a hallway at the top of the room
- Gravity changes erratically as you move about the room making flying/jumping from stone-to-stone both required and difficult
- 3x Huge Gravity Elementals lurk among the debris

## Mechanics

The hard way up:
- Jumping from chunk to chunk is a crapshoot; normally, an Acrobatics check (DC 15) would be sufficient to make the leap but the rapid fluctuations in gravity give each attempt a 25% miss chance. Each piece of debris is 20ft, 40ft, and 60ft off the ground, respectively.
- Upon successfully jumping, the player must make a climb check (DC 12) to climb to the top of the debris section

The easy way up:
- Flying. A DC15 fly check is required each time the gravity fluctuates (3x 1/4 chances on the way up)

## Encounters
3x Huge Gravity Elementals await the PCs here
