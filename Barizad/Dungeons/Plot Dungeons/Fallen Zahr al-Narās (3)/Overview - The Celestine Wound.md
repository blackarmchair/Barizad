## Theme
This is not merely a ruin — it is the open wound of the Brass City, where the Celestine Furnace overheated, fractured, and bled prophecy into unreality. The laws of time, identity, and logic warp here. The deeper the players go, the more they walk not just through space, but across discarded futures.

### What This Level Represents
- The place Sirr al-Nar severed fate and tried to rewrite it
- The living core of the fallen city's oracular engine
- A prison, a relic, a forge: wrapped into a broken metaphysical machine
- Here, true dreams leak, and unlived possibilities weep
