## Room Layout

A vast, circular chamber (100 ft diameter), three stories high. Cells ring the outer wall like honeycomb segments, stacked across all levels. A central tower-platform rises 20 feet above the floor with a spiral ramp, containing a control dais of gears and levers, half rusted, half still humming.

- Total of 8 visible cells, each behind shimmering brass runic wards
- A few are empty, others glow faintly, or whisper
- Above each cell is a broken inscription panel showing Celestine glyphs — several of which flicker, mistranslate, or contradict themselves

## Description

> Brass railings groan as the spiral ramp carries you to the heart of the panopticon. The cells along the perimeter pulse with old power.
> 
> From above, a voice not your own whispers: “Sentenced for deviation. Awaiting fate adjustment.”
> 
> The levers before you bear no names — only shifting sigils that change when you look away.

## Mechanics

There are 16 levers oriented around a generally circular center console. Each lever bears a letter on the tip. Wherever the PCs decide to start, roll 2d8 to determine the starting position. The letters are as follows: g-n-i-h-t-o-n-r-o-g-n-i-h-t-y-r-e-v-e.

Pulling a lever (or levers) on its own does nothing. A large red button sits in the center of the console; pushing this button activates the mechanism with an effect based on which lever(s) the PCs have pulled.

Following is a list of effects:

| Lever(s) Pulled                   | Effect                                                                                                          |
| --------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Any lever(s) marked e, E, o, or O | Springs a Pit Trap (CR 5) (DMG pg. 72) on one of the marked squares for each lever pulled (determined randomly) |
| Any other lever(s)                | Opens a number of cell doors equal to the number of non-trap levers pulled.                                     |
| All levers                        | The exit door unlocks and swings open                                                                           |

### Horde of Valuables (1)
A brass chest containing:
- 1 Potion of Protection from Fire
- 1 Ring of Protection (+4)
- 1 Rod of Thunder and Lightning

### Horde of Valuables (2)

- 1 Rod of Python

- 1 Belt of Giant's Strength (+4)

### Horde of Valuables (3)
A grand and intricate True Brass chest (itself worth Xgp) containing:
- 1 Darkwood Buckler

	- True Seeing (CL 9th)
	- Magic Circle Against Chaos (CL 5th)
	- Divine Power (CL 7th)
- 1 Sword of Subtlety
- 1 Carpet of Flying (10' x 10')
