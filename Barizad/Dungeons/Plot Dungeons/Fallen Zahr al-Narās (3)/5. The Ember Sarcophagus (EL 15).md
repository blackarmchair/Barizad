## Room Layout
- A circular vault (80 ft diameter) with walls scorched black and glowing with subtle runes
- In the center lies a massive sarcophagus-shaped construct, suspended mid-air by chains of glass and true brass
- Dream-gem shards orbit slowly around it, refracting unreal light — a rainbow of futures that never were
- A spiral walkway on the far side of the room descends from to a lower platform beneath the sarcophagus (40 ft drop)

## Description

> You feel it before you see it: a weight behind your thoughts, like someone else’s destiny leaning against your own. Then the light changes — thick, flickering, as though passing through oil — and the chamber opens below.
> 
> Suspended in the air by glass-threaded chains is a sarcophagus of brass and obsidian, nearly twenty feet long. It pulses softly, like breath. Around it orbit six dream-gems, flickering with scenes too fast to follow — lives unlived.
> 
> The runes on the walls read: “That which cannot be must be forgotten.”
## Mechanics
As the PCs approach (or if they attempt to interact with the floating dream gem shards):
- The floating shards snap together into a single gem and hover at the center of the sarcophagus
- The sarcophagus itself whirs and whines from inside as moving parts once too smooth and well-integrated to be seen become visible. It begins taking the shape of a clockwork construct.
- The construct is twice the height of a man and more than twice his bulk. Its obsidian skin seems no less armored than the true brass plates that partially cover it. Its fists crackle with energy. Its eyes are too far sunken into an ornate helmet to be seen; only 2 tiny red gleams belie their presence at all. In the center of its chest, the dream gem pulses and glows.
- In a booming voice that echoes a fraction of a second later inside your mind:  
    "Deviation is not permitted. This outcome was not sanctioned."

### Combat with the Marut
See MM page 159.

The guardian here is a typical Marut but with the unique qualities:
- This Marut is designed to punish those who resist the fate written for them
- 1/encounter can declare a single PC as "discordant"; that PC has a -2 penalty on Will saves against the Marut's SLAs
- 1/encounter can use Dominate Person as a SLA against a PC bearing a dream gem. The dream gem manipulation causes a -4 penalty to the save
