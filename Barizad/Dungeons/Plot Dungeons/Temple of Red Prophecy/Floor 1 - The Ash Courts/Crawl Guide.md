# Temple of Red Prophecy (Floor 1) — Crawl Guide
*Levels 8–9 | EL 7–9 | Party of Four*

## General Features

**Construction Material.** Volcanic sacred architecture — walls of rough-hewn obsidian fitted with copper fixtures, floors of compressed volcanic ash reinforced with brass grating. Fire gutters run along every corridor: shallow channels filled with burning tallow mixed with sacred oils. Doorways are arched, 8 feet tall, carved with Zanethi glyphs depicting the Chained Sun. Nothing is built for comfort — this is a place of devotion, not habitation.

**Ceiling Height.** Corridors are 8 feet high with low-hanging copper censers every 20 feet. The Courtyard of Ashes is open-air. The Gate of Burning Testimony has a 15-foot vaulted ceiling. The Smoke Refectory is 10 feet with heavy smoke pooling at the ceiling. The Branding Hall is 12 feet with chains and hooks hanging from iron crossbeams. The Throat of Cinders is a 150-foot vertical shaft.

**Lighting.** Dim light throughout — fire gutters provide flickering illumination equivalent to candlelight (shadowy illumination to 10 feet). The Courtyard is lit by the open sky and a bonfire altar. The Smoke Refectory is heavily obscured by incense (20% miss chance beyond 5 feet). The Throat of Cinders is lit from below by volcanic glow.

## Crawl Strategy Notes

**Marching Order.** Corridors are 10 feet wide — double-file is standard. The Throat of Cinders narrows to 5 feet at points during the descent, forcing single-file. The Courtyard is open enough for any formation.

**Visibility.** The constant smoke and incense impose -2 on Spot checks in corridors. The Smoke Refectory is worse: -4 on Spot and 20% miss chance beyond 5 feet. The Courtyard has clear visibility but is overlooked by the upper terrace (sentries have line of sight). Darkvision functions normally but does not negate smoke penalties.

**Spot/Listen.** Note each PC's modifiers. Key thresholds:
- Spot +6 or better: notices the Azer sentries' patrol pattern at the Gate before entering visual range, giving time to plan
- Listen +8 or better: hears the chanting from the Courtyard bonfire two rooms away, indicating the number and fervor of the worshippers
- Spot +10 or better: notices the hidden passage in the Smoke Refectory wall (Search DC 20 to open) without dedicating a full stretch to searching
- Listen +12 or better: hears Thorrak's animal companion shifting in the Throat of Cinders from the Branding Hall, warning the party of a guardian ahead

## Stretch Pacing

The five chambers are arranged around the volcanic rift's upper terrace. Travelling between connected chambers costs 1 stretch per corridor. The party enters through the Gate and must reach the Throat of Cinders to descend.

### Room-by-Room Estimates

| Chamber | Estimated Stretches | Key Timing |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Corridors (between chambers) | 1 each | Roll for wandering encounters. Smoke and fire-gutter light. |
| The Gate of Burning Testimony (EL 7) | 1–2 | Combat or stealth entry. Crossing the sacrificial trench takes 1 round (Jump DC 12) or 1 stretch (find the bridge). |
| The Courtyard of Ashes (EL 8) | 2–4 | Volcanic vent eruptions every 3 stretches. Intervening with the bound sacrifice adds 1–2 stretches. Combat with berserkers is round-scale. |
| The Smoke Refectory (EL 8) | 2–3 | Fortitude saves every stretch against narcotic incense. Finding the hidden passage requires 1 stretch of searching. Avoiding violence allows a shortcut to Floor 2. |
| The Branding Hall (EL 9) | 2–3 | Combat encounter with Flame-Taken sergeant. Examining wall carvings takes 1 stretch (Decipher Script DC 18 or Comprehend Languages). |
| The Throat of Cinders (EL 8) | 2–4 | Climb/rope descent (Climb DC 18, 150 feet). Thorrak encounter at the midpoint. |

**Total estimated stretches for Floor 1:** 12–20 (2–3.5 hours of in-game time)

### Relevant Activities (3.5e)

- **Search Area.** Essential in the Smoke Refectory (Search DC 20 for the hidden passage) and the Gate (Search DC 15 for the bridge mechanism). Searching the Branding Hall wall carvings yields lore about Zaneth's "exile."
- **Keep Watch.** Critical in the Courtyard (sentries patrol on a schedule) and during the Throat descent (Thorrak may ambush climbers mid-rope).
- **Take Your Time.** Taking 20 on the Smoke Refectory search (DC 20) is possible but costs 2 stretches and risks additional Fortitude saves against the incense. Taking 20 on Climb checks (DC 18) in the Throat is possible but costs 4 stretches for the full descent.
- **Helping Out.** Aid Another on Climb checks is the intended design for the Throat. Aid Another on Diplomacy checks with the acolytes in the Smoke Refectory can shift the encounter toward peaceful passage.
- **Casting a Spell.** Ritual casting (10 min) fits into 1 stretch. Relevant rituals: Comprehend Languages (to read the Branding Hall carvings), Detect Magic (to examine the Gate's fire gutters for magical traps), Neutralize Poison or Delay Poison (to resist the Smoke Refectory incense).

## Wandering Monster Table

Wandering monsters patrol the corridors of the Ash Courts. They are temple defenders on regular patrol routes — not random creatures but organized sentries responding to disturbances.

**Encounter Frequency.** Medium — roll 1d6 at the end of every 3rd stretch.
**Encounter Likelihood.** Possible — on a 5 or 6, roll on the table.
**Encounter Difficulty.** Moderate — Floor 1 encounters should cost resources and signal that the Temple is actively defended, but not overwhelm a party that is pacing itself.
