**Based on:** Original concept
 
**Dungeon Concept:** An impossible lake of still, reflective mercury. Drifting islands move like thoughts. The PCs’ beliefs alter the fabric of the dungeon.
 
**Dungeon Highlights:**

- Illusions become real if player's believe they're real
- A magical telescope floats in the air, observing intruders
 
**Dream-Gem:** _Reflexive Destiny_
