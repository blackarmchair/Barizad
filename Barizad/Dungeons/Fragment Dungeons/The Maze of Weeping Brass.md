**Based on:** _The Tale of the Three Apples_  
******Location:** A submerged labyrinth beneath a fractured coastal ruin
 
**Theme:** Tragedy, consequence, repetition
 
**Premise:**  
A vault once used to weigh divine judgments replays a single crime endlessly. The PCs must follow clues through a murder-mystery while brass ghosts relive the tragedy, becoming increasingly aware of their loop. The dream-gem is hidden inside the heart of the victim—or the killer.
 
**Dungeon Features:**

- Ghosts trapped in emotional routines (investigation and roleplay focus)
- Waterlogged rooms where guilt and sorrow manifest as physical hazards
- Boss fight: a brass inquisitor animated by conflicting verdicts
