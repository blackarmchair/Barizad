**Based on:** _The Tale of the Astrologer Who Fell Into a Cistern_
 
**Location:** A shattered observatory vault suspended in a gravity-warped ravine
 
**Theme:** Misinterpretation, hubris, divine irony
 
**Premise:**  
An ancient astrologer sought to chart all possible futures—but now his shattered observatory projects contradictory constellations and fate-paths. The dungeon cycles through false “prophetic” outcomes, each a mini-zone with challenges based on alternate destinies. The astrologer’s fractured soul animates a giant brass orrery at the center, and must be confronted or re-aligned.
 
**Dungeon Features:**

- Prophetic illusions: players experience fake “futures”
- Star-tracking puzzle requiring correct planetary alignment
- Final chamber rotates during combat, altering time flow and spell durations
