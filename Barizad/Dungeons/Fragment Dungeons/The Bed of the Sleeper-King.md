**Based on:** _The Tale of the Sleeper and the Waker_
 
**Location:** A fallen brass spire lying on its side in a deep jungle
 
**Theme:** Stasis vs awakening, fate deferred, slumbering catastrophe
 
**Premise:**  
Inside the tilted halls of this fallen fragment lies a **sleeping efreet prince**, surrounded by time-locked servants. His dreams shape reality: the dungeon changes based on what part of his dream is dominant. The PCs must wake or kill him—but doing so may trigger the return of a major threat.
 
**Dungeon Features:**

- Rooms shift when players sleep, rest, or cast dream-related magic
- Dream-gem protected by paradoxical guardians: awake but unaware, or asleep and hostile
- Final chamber: battle in shifting dream-states, with fate dice dictating terrain or initiative order
