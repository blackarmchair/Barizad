**Based on:** _The Tale of the Ebony Horse_
 
**Dungeon Concept:** A warped remnant of the Brass City’s war-stables. Brass steeds roam, and a war-ifrit still clings to duty and purity of prophecy.
 
**Dungeon Highlights:**

- Construct steeds with unstable AI; ride or disable
- Final boss: General Safraz atop a flaming brass nightmare
 
**Dream-Gem:** _The Loyalty That Outlived Kings_
