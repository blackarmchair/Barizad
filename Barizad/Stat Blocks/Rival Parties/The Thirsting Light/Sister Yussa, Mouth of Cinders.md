## Visual and Flavor

- Wears a half-mask of scorched porcelain, cracked at the mouth
- Speaks softly, but her words burn like scripture
- Was once a noble from Varnash, gave her name and family to the Temple in exchange for prophecy

## Tactics

- Begins flying high with See the Unseen and Dark One's Own Luck active
- Drops **Chilling Tentacles** in midst of enemy ranks or to split battlefield
- Uses Quicken Eldritch Blast + Frightful or Vitriolic combo on vulnerable PCs
- Engages support casters or ranged characters from above
- Will flee if reduced below 25% HP unless Naehri is still standing
