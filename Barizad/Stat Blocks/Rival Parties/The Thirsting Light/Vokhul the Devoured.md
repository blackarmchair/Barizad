*"Each scar speaks. Each burn remembers."*

## Visual and Flavor

- Burn-scarred torso etched with black-inked glyphs of Zaneth's prophecy
- Teeth filed. Left eye replaced with a seared brass disc engraved with a broken prophecy line
- Speaks in short, declarative phrases. Believes his rage is sacred communion

## Tactics

- **Opener:** Activates Rage + Smite + Divine Might + charges using Pounce
- **Main Combo:** Full-attack with Power Attack (-2 to hit, +4 dmg) on each strike
- **Target Priorities:** Priests, prophets, paladins, or Myrian foes
- **Recklessness:** Will fight through Prayer, Bestow Curse, or Flame Strike effects if "the Vision" demands it
