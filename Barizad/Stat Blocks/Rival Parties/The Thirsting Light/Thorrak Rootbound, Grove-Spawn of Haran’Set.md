## Visual and Flavor

- Bark-covered skin; fungus colonies grow from his shoulders and crawl like insects
- Eyes milky green. Voice like wet leaves tearing
- Beliefs: all empires rot. Haran'Set is the proof. The gems are germination — fate is compost

## Tactics

- Opens with Entangle, Obscuring Mist, or Summon Swarm
- Turns into Dire Wolf to flank with companion for trip/sneak combo
- Uses Poison and Contagion on martial characters
- Spams Summon Nature's Ally IV with Greenbound for aggressive battlefield saturation
- Retreats only to preserve the seed-body for rebirth
