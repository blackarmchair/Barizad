## Visual and Flavor

- Blind (self-inflicted); eyes burned shut with incense-charcoal, replaced with dream-sight
- Hair shorn, robes always slightly smoking
- Speaks in riddled aphorisms: "The smoke that blinds the seer clears the way for truth."

## Tactics

- Begins with Obscuring Mist or Silence to control space
- Targets Lawful or Myrian PCs with Hold Person into Searing Light
- Uses **Discordant Chant** to ruin time-sensitive PC tactics
- Activates **Iconoclast's Rebuttal** against Hold, Charm, or Fear
- Sacrifices lower-level spells to force rerolls via **Inversion Doctrine**
