## Visual and Flavor

- Towering, shirtless warrior in scorched brass lamellar. His falchion glows with ashfire runes, and his body bears ritual scarring in patterns that flicker with unread fate-scripts.
- Once a desert champion sworn to protect the weak, Bramm's fate was rewritten by a dream-gem seeded with Sirr al-Nar's will. His past is gone — burned clean. His future? Singular and absolute.

## Tactics

- **Opener:** Activates Rage + Smite + Divine Might + charges using Pounce
- **Main Combo:** Full-attack with Power Attack (-2 to hit, +4 dmg) on each strike
- **Target Priorities:** Priests, prophets, paladins, or Myrian foes
- **Recklessness:** Will fight through Prayer, Bestow Curse, or Flame Strike effects if "the Vision" demands it
