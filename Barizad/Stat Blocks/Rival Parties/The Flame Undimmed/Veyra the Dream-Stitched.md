## Visual and Flavor

- Sirr al-Nar loyalist. Her left eye socket holds a dream-gem that pulses faintly when she speaks.
- Moves with preternatural calm. Smiles too easily. When she casts, the gem flares and her voice doubles — her own and something older.

## Tactics

- Begins combat with Mage Armor and Mirror Image pre-cast
- Uses Displacement or Greater Invisibility before moving to control the battlefield
- Opens with Slow, Suggestion, or Fell Draining Ray of Enfeeblement
- Keeps Phantasmal Killer in reserve for isolated PCs or wounded targets
- If cornered: Blindness/Deafness then Expeditious Retreat to flee
- Will never use lethal force unless ordered by Seraphine or the Furnace pulses
