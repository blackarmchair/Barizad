## Tactics

- Opens with Shield of Faith, Silence, or Hold Person
- Uses Flame Strike or Dispel Magic to neutralize enemy buffs
- Targets paladins, Lawful outsiders, or Myrian PCs first
- Activates **Discordant Chant** to ruin timing-based tactics
- **Iconoclast's Rebuttal** triggers on charm/daze/fear spells — Confusion retaliation
- Uses Mark of Justice for long-term punishment if a PC flees
- Retreats only if Bramm is down or her holy symbol is shattered
