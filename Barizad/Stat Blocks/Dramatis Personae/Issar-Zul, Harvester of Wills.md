## Tactics

- **Opener**: _Greater Command_ + _Blade Barrier_ to divide the field
- **Mid-fight**: Casts _Flame Strike_ and uses **Chains of False Revelation** to pin down high-threat PCs

- May _disguise self_ or vanish entirely via domain spells if about to be defeated
