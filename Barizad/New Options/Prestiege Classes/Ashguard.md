_Elite warrior-sentinel bonded to a relic of True Brass.____(_Martial tank/anti-mage; battlefield denial)  
Clad in sacred armor and oath-bound to relics, the Ashguard fights to uphold the dignity of imperial law.
 
**Hit Die**: d10
 
**Requirements:**  
To qualify to become an Ashguard, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
 
**Race**: any  
**Base Attack Bonus**: +6  
**Proficiency**: Heavy Armor and Martial Weapons  
**Special:** Must be attuned to a True Brass relic (armor or weapon)
 
**Class Skills:**  
The Ashguard's class skills are Climb, Craft (Armorsmithing or Weaponsmithing), Intimidate, Knowledge (Nobility), Knowledge (Religion), Ride, Sense Motive  
**Skill Points at Each Level**: 2 + Int modifier.

Class Features
 
**Brassbound Endurance (Ex)**: While wearing or wielding a True Brass relic, reduce damage taken from divine spells, smite attacks, or channel energy by 5 (stacking with resistance effects).
 
**Smite the Unworthy (Su)**: Once per day (twice at 5th level), you may declare an attack against an Outsider or Undead as a smite. Add your Charisma bonus to the attack roll and your class level to damage.
 
**Relic Welding (Su)**: You may bind a minor magical item (or True Brass fragment) into your armor or weapon. This frees the magic item’s normal slot (e.g., cloak, ring) and suppresses its abilities unless the Ashguard is wielding or wearing the welded relic. Effects are always passive and cannot require activation. You gain 1 slot at 2nd level and a second at 4th.
 
**Indomitable Pride (Ex)**: Allies adjacent to you gain a +1 morale bonus on Will saves. This increases to +2 against fear or compulsion effects. You are immune to any effect that would cause you to kneel, fall prone, or otherwise lose stature unless you allow it.
 
**True Brass Awakening (Su)****:** When reduced below 0 HP, you may make a Fortitude save (DC = damage taken). On success, you remain at 1 HP and gain +2 AC and +2 saves for 1 minute. This may occur once per day and only while wearing or wielding a True Brass item.
