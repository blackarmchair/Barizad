_Radical devotee who shapes reality through disruptive faith.____(_Chaotic divine caster; battlefield control; anti-fate zealot)  
These clerics twist prophecy into weaponized doubt. They chant inverse litanies that jam the gears of fate.

- **Prereqs:** Worship Zaneth, Knowledge (Religion) 8, Chaotic alignment
- **Abilities:**
    
    - Once/encounter: prevent an enemy from taking readied or delayed actions
    - Sacrifice a spell slot to force reroll on a save against confusion or illusion
    - Can cast _dispel law_ once/day, even if it’s not on your spell list
 
**Hit Die**: d6
 
**Requirements:**  
To qualify to become a Hierarch of Zaneth, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
 
**Race**: any  
**Region**: Aram-Zaneth  
**Alignment**: Any Chaotic  
**Skills:** Knowledge (Religion) 8 ranks  
**Spellcasting**: Must be able to cast divine spells  
**Special**: Must worship Zaneth and have publicly rejected an ordained fate (subject to DM approval)
   

**Class Skills:**  
The Hierarch of Zaneth's class skills are Bluff, Concentration, Disguise, Intimidate, Knowledge (Religion), Sense Motive, Spellcraft  
**Skill Points at Each Level**: 4 + Int modifier.

Class Features
 
**Dispel Order (Sp)**: You may cast *dispel law* once per day, even if it is not on your spell list. At 4th level, you may use it twice per day.
 
**Discordant Chant (Su)**: Once per encounter, you may chant a paradoxical litany that prevents a single enemy (within 30 ft, Will negates DC 10 + class level + Cha mod) from taking readied or delayed actions for 1 round. Creatures immune to mind-affecting effects are immune to this.
 
**Inversion Doctrine (Su)**: You may sacrifice a divine spell slot as a free action to force an enemy to reroll a failed saving throw against an illusion or confusion effect cast by you. The second roll must be taken.
 
**Liturgical Schism (Ex)**: Your utterances disrupt divine certainty. Enemies within 10 ft take a -2 penalty on saves against your divine spells if they are Lawful or have spell resistance.
 
**Iconoclast’s Rebuttal (Su)**: Once per day, when you are affected by a mind-affecting or compulsion effect, you may immediately force the source to make a Will save (DC 10 + class level + your Cha mod) or be affected by *confusion* for 1 round.
