_Voice of the dead and guardian of stellar passage.___(Spirit-talker, anti-undead support, dream-magic specialist)  
Tomb-Speakers walk between embalming halls and astral altars. They “interpret” the lingering wills of the entombed.  
**Hit Die**: d6
 
**Requirements:**  
To qualify to become a Tomb-Speaker, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
 
**Race**: any  
**Alignment**: Any Lawful  
**Skills:** Knowledge (Religion) 8 ranks  
**Spells**: Ability to cast (or otherwise use) _Speak with Dead_  
**Attributes**: Cha 13  
**Special**: Must have performed a funerary rite for a noble or royal.
 
**Class Skills:**  
The Tomb-Speaker’s class skills (and the key ability for each skill) are Concentration, Diplomacy, Heal, Knowledge (Religion), Knowledge (The Planes), Listen, Sense Motive, and Spellcraft  
**Skill Points at Each Level**: 4 + Int modifier. ****

**Class Features**:
 
**Royal Communion (Su)**: Once per day, you may cast _Speak with Dead_ as a supernatural ability without material components. When used in a royal tomb or shrine, you may ask 1 additional question.
 
**Astral Consecration (Su)**: You may consecrate a room or chamber under open sky or tomb ceiling. While within this area, you and allies gain +1 sacred bonus to saves vs fear and death effects.
 
**Soulward Insight (Ex)**: +2 bonus on saves against death effects and +2 on Sense Motive checks vs undead or possessed creatures.
 
**Celestial Turning (Su)**: You gain the ability to turn or rebuke undead depending on the current celestial phase (day/night or DM-determined). Day = Turn; Night = Rebuke.
 
**Tomb-Rite Casting (Sp)**: At levels 4, 6, and 8, you may perform a special funerary ritual (1 hour) to gain a 1/day spell-like ability:  
- **4th**: _Sending_  
- **6th**: _Dream_  
- **8th**: _Ethereal Jaunt_ (self only)
 
**Tomb-Ward (Su)**: You and allies within 30 ft of a tomb you gain +2 AC against and cannot be flanked by undead or incorporeal creatures.
 
**Starborne Vigil (Ex)**: You no longer need to sleep; your dreams become prophetic. You are immune to magical sleep and may use _Augury_ 1/day as spell-like ability.
 
**Radiant Ghostsight (Su)**: You can see invisible, ethereal, and/or incorporeal undead at all times, as if under the effects of _See Invisibility_. This does not reveal other invisible creatures.
 
**Royal Consultation (Su)**: Once per week, you may attempt to speak to the soul of a long-dead king or queen. This functions as _Greater Planar Ally_ (undead or celestial ancestor only), but no payment is required if it is used to protect their tomb, legacy, or bloodline.
