_Nomadic seer who sings the signs of the storm and sand._  
Zifari wind-callers interpret divine signs through dust devils, the shifting moon, or the cracking of salt pans.  
**Hit Die**: d8
 
**Requirements:**  
To qualify to become a Caller of the Wind Shrines, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
 
**Race**: any  
**Skills:** Survival 8 ranks, Perform (Oratory or Cant) 4 ranks  
**Special**: Must have received a ritual blessing at a recognized wind-shrine of the Zifari  
**Class**: at least one level in Bard
 
**Class Skills:**  
The Caller of the Wind Shrines' class skills are Concentration, Handle Animal, Knowledge (Nature), Knowledge (Religion), Perform (Oratory), Sense Motive, Spellcraft, Survival  
**Skill Points at Each Level**: 6 + Int modifier.

Class Features
 
**Voice of Dust and Gale (Su)**: Once per day, you may cast _Augury_ or _Wind Wall_ as a supernatural ability without material components. At 4th level, you may use this ability twice per day.
 
**Wind-Ward (Su)**: As a standard action, you may invoke the blessing of a wind-shrine to create a protective current around yourself or an ally within 30 feet. This grants a +2 deflection bonus to AC and a +2 bonus on Reflex saves for 1 minute. In addition, the target gains concealment (20% miss chance) against ranged attacks made from more than 30 feet away. This ability may be used once per day at 1st level, and twice per day at 4th level.
 
**Weather-Touched Verse (Su)**: You may treat one spell per level that appears on the druid or cleric spell list with the Air or Weather descriptor as a bard spell known. You must still have the appropriate spell level to cast it. This selection is permanent once chosen.
 
**Desert Signs (Ex)**: You gain a +4 bonus on saving throws against illusions or magical effects that obscure paths, directions, or natural terrain. You may also roll twice and take the better result when using Survival to avoid becoming lost.
 
**Gale-Seer’s Clarity (Su)**: Once per day, you may enter a trance for 1 minute, gaining the effects of _True Seeing_ and _Find the Path_ for the duration. During this time, you must sing or chant continuously, and cannot be silenced or deafened.
