_Archivist-priest trained to translate Celestine fragments._  
(Lore-based spellcaster; scroll-crafter; tactical support)
 
Tablet-Keepers aren’t just scribes—they _negotiate_ with knowledge itself, decoding the lawful scripts of a ruined heaven.__  
**Hit Die**: d6
 
**Requirements:**  
To qualify to become a Tablet-Keeper, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
 
**Race**: any  
**Alignment**: Any Lawful  
**Class**: Cleric  
**Skills:** Knowledge (Arcana or Religion) 8 ranks  
**Spells**: Ability to cast (or otherwise use) _Comprehend Languages_
 
**Class Skills:**  
The Tablet-Keeper’s class skills are Concentration, Decipher Script, Knowledge (Arcana), Knowledge (History), Knowledge (Religion), Spellcraft, and Use Magic Device  
**Skill Points at Each Level**: 4 + Int modifier.

Class Features
 
**Scribe Celestine Fragment (Ex)**: You may scribe special scrolls from Celestine Fragments (DMG or unique ruins). These scrolls may cast any divine spell you know of 3rd level or lower, but each fragment can only hold one spell at a time and degrades after 3 uses.
 
**Fragment Literacy (Ex)**: You gain a +4 bonus on Decipher Script and Use Magic Device checks involving Celestine or magical texts. You may use _Comprehend Languages_ as a spell-like ability once per day.
 
**Domain Infusion (Su)**: Choose one additional domain from Brass, Law, or Knowledge. You gain access to its domain spells and granted power as if granted by your deity.
 
**Scripted Insight (Su)**: Once per day, after spending 10 minutes with a magical or prophetic inscription, you may gain a +2 bonus on all saving throws for 1 hour, or grant that bonus to an ally who carries the transcription.
 
**Fragmentary Oracle (Su)**: Once per day, you may touch a willing creature and read their fate. This functions as _Augury_ (80% success chance), but with the option to ask one Yes/No question instead of the usual outcome format.
