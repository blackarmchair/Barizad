_You have trained to navigate tides of unreality and vision._
 
**Prerequisite:** Cha 13, Region: Nimari, Zifari
 
**Benefit:** +2 bonus on saves vs illusions and sleep effects. You may reroll one failed Listen, Sense Motive, or Spot check per day.
 
**Special:** You may take 10 on Balance checks even in uncertain or dreamlike terrain.
