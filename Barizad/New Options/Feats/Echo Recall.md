_Your memory skips like a stone across time._
 
**Prerequisite:** Int 13
 
**Benefit:** Once per day, you may immediately recast a spell you just cast. You cast this spell exactly as though it were a spell you prepared but it does not take-up a spell slot. Must be 2nd level or lower.
 
**Special:** This does not provoke an attack of opportunity, but both versions share the same save DC and target.
