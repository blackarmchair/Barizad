_You bear the spiritual imprint of the Celestine Machine._
 
**Prerequisite:** Region: Zahr al-Narās or descendant of Hollowforged
 
**Benefit:** You gain a +1 bonus on saves against compulsion and force effects. You may stabilize automatically at –1 HP once per day.
 
**Special:** You may be treated as a construct or humanoid (whichever is less favorable) for spells and effects.
