_You’ve studied the harmonics of fate through coded prophecy._
 
**Prerequisite:** Int 13, Knowledge (Religion) 4 ranks, Myrian Halls background (or DM permission)
 
**Benefit:** 1/day, when you or an ally within 30 ft would reroll a save or check, you may allow them to roll twice and take the better result.
 
**Special:** You take a –2 penalty to Will saves for 1 round after using this ability.
