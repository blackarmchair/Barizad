Each time standing with a faction increases, standing with listed opposing factions drops by 1, unless mitigated by clever diplomacy or deception.
 
Standing should change based on:

- Major story choices (siding in conflicts, fulfilling quests, slaying or sparing key NPCs).
- Cultural respect (performing sacred rites correctly, speaking truthfully, bearing symbols).
- Betrayals, insults, or violations of law/taboo.
 
Each tier of standing grants a benefit:

|   |   |
|---|---|
|**Standing Tier**|**Example Benefits**|
|+1 Trusted|Lower prices, safe passage, minor favors.|
|+3 Honored Ally|Military aid, rare information, elite guides or mounts.|
|+5 Favored|Access to sacred sites, dream-gem gifts, rituals, even temporary divine aid.|
|-1 Distrusted|Minor indignities, general bad attitude|
|–3 Despised|Watched, overcharged, given false information.|
|–5 Hunted|Open attacks, cursed, marked for dream-hunters or assassins.|
